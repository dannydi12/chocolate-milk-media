About Us

Looking for some help on your next production? At Chocolate Milk Media, film is at the core of everything we do. We are a group of passionate creatives looking to engage with other creators and the larger community. Drop us a line to have us work on your next project.

  • Madison De La Garza

    From a young age, Madison De La Garza expressed her passion for social awareness through film, writing her first titled "The Imbalancing Act" at 14. Inspired by her hero, Rod Serling, she then wrote and produced her sci-fi short film "Subject 16” the following year. Madison is currently a student at Chapman University’s Dodge College of Film and Media Arts and will soon be making her co-directorial debut alongside another one of her inspirations, Maddy Murden, on their film “Pink Elephant” in January 2020.

    IMDb Instagram

  • Sam Gilbert

    Sam Gilbert is a cinematographer and camera operator. He is ARRI certified in their ALEXA and AMIRA camera and lighting systems as well as being a member of the Society of Camera Operators. After being an actor for 10 years, having acted in movies and shows like “New Girl”, “The Middle”, and “Conan”, he decided to move behind the camera. Sam has worked on projects with almost every camera system including: ARRI, RED, Black Magic, Canon, and Panasonic cinema cameras.

    IMDb Instagram

  • Madeleine Noel Murden

    Madeleine Noel Murden has been working professionally in the industry since the age of twelve, and has since adopted an incurable love for film. She is a proud recipient of a National YoungArts Foundation award in Cinematic Arts and is currently cycling several short films through a number of festivals, with recent success at the Poppy Jasper International Film Festival and the Clean Shorts Film Festival. She has directed a number of music videos and is currently editing “Pink Elephant”, a film which she wrote, co-directed, and starred in.

    IMDb Instagram

  • Will Murden

    Will has worked on a number of productions, from short films to music videos. Much of his creative work can be seen on the YouTube channel “MadsWill”, a project run by both siblings. He can be seen in episodic works such as “Manhunt: Unabomber”, “This Close”, and the short film “Everything I Am”.

    IMDb Instagram

  • Daniel DiVenere

    Daniel DiVenere is an FAA-licensed drone operator, actor, and musician. He has spent over 10 years in the industry, becoming closely aware of the inner-workings of film and television while also pursuing his other passions. In his spare time, Daniel can be found songwriting, programming, or pondering Elon Musk’s god-like existence.
