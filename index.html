About Us

Looking for some help on your next production? At Chocolate Milk Media, film is at the core of everything we do. We are a group of passionate creatives looking to engage with other creators and the larger community. Drop us a line to have us work on your next project.

  • Joe Doe

    Madison De La Garza spent early childhood among a diverse Tejano culture before moving to Los Angeles to play the daughter of Eva Longoria on ABC’s Desperate Housewives. From a young age she expressed her passion for social awareness through film, writing her first, "The Imbalancing Act" at 14. Inspired by her hero, Rod Serling, she then wrote and produced her sci-fi short film "Subject 16” the following year. Madison is currently a student at Chapman University’s Dodge College of Film and Media Arts and will soon be making her co-directorial debut alongside another one of her inspirations, Maddy Murden, on their film “Pink Elephant” in January 2020.
